# my saves

A minimal, privacy-first tool for organising your LinkedIn saved posts.
All data lives in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server.
Features

Import saved posts via JSON scraper
AI categorisation using Claude
Custom buckets to organise posts
Star, pin, archive, and annotate posts
Light and dark mode
Works on mobile

How to use

Open the site
Click Import Saves and select your scraped JSON file
Switch to Uncategorised and hit ✦ AI Categorise
Browse and manage your posts by bucket

Data & privacy
Your posts are stored in your browser's localStorage only. No account, no backend, no tracking.
